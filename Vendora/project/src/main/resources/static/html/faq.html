Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to your concerns about Vendora

1. Do I need an account to place an order?
Yes, you need to create an account to place orders and track your purchases securely.
2. How do I place an order?
Simply browse our products, add your desired items to the cart, and proceed to checkout to complete your purchase.
3. What payment methods are available?
We accept all major Credit/Debit Cards and offer Cash on Delivery (COD) for your convenience.
4. How can I track my order?
Once logged in, you can track the status of your package through the "Track Orders" section in your profile.
5. Can I cancel or modify my order?
Yes, orders can be canceled or modified through your dashboard as long as the status has not yet changed to "Shipped."
6. How long does delivery take?
Delivery typically takes between 3–5 working days, depending on your specific location within the country.
7. What if I receive a damaged or wrong product?
Please contact our support team immediately via the Service Page to request a replacement or refund.
8. Are the products authentic?
Absolutely. All products sold on Vendora are 100% genuine and sourced directly from verified, trusted suppliers.
9. Can I return a product?
Yes, returns are accepted within our specified window (see Terms) if the product is unopened and in its original packaging.
10. How can I contact customer support?
You can reach us through the contact form on our Services page or by emailing us directly at the address listed below.
