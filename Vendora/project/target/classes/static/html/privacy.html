Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide and improve our services, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, and residential/delivery addresses.
  • Account Information: Login credentials, preferences, and profile data.
  • Transaction Data: Order details, payment method (excluding sensitive payment credentials), purchase history, and applied discounts or vouchers.
  • Uploaded Content: Images, documents, or other files submitted by users (e.g., for returns, verification, or support requests).
  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and browsing activity on the website.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and manage orders, payments, and deliveries accurately and efficiently.
  • To communicate with users regarding account activity, orders, returns, support, and promotional offers (only if opted in).
  • To improve platform functionality, features, and user experience through analytics.
  • To prevent fraudulent, illegal, or unauthorized activities on the platform.
  • To comply with legal obligations, including record keeping for business and tax purposes.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • Customer information (name, phone, delivery address) is shared with delivery personnel strictly for order fulfillment.
  • Suppliers may access limited data relevant to product orders they need to fulfill.
  • We may share information with trusted third-party service providers, such as payment gateways, IT service providers, or analytics platforms, solely to provide services.
  • We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information to third parties.
  • We may disclose information if required by law, to protect our rights, or in case of a legal investigation.

Data Security

  • We implement appropriate technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect user data.
  • Passwords are stored securely using hashing and encryption techniques.
  • While we strive to protect data, no system is completely secure; users provide personal data at their own risk.
  • Access to user data by internal staff is restricted based on necessity and role.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

  • We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, maintain login sessions, and analyze usage patterns.
  • Cookies help track order progress, session activity, and website analytics.
  • Users may manage or disable cookies through browser settings; however, some features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

User Rights

  • Users can access, update, or correct their personal information at any time through their account settings.
  • Users may request deletion of their account and personal data in accordance with platform policies.
  • Users can opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Users have the right to request information about how their data is used and who it is shared with.

Communication

  • Vendora communicates with users through email, SMS, or approved messaging channels regarding orders, account notifications, and support.
  • All orders include tracking numbers to monitor delivery progress.
  • We ensure that communication is relevant, timely, and secure.

Data Retention

  • We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill business operations, legal obligations, and dispute resolution.
  • Transaction and account data may be archived for record-keeping and auditing purposes.
  • Inactive accounts may be deactivated or deleted according to platform retention policies.

Third-Party Services

  • The platform may integrate with third-party services such as payment gateways, analytics tools, or delivery tracking systems.
  • These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies, and Vendora is not responsible for their practices.
  • We carefully select partners and ensure that they meet minimum security and privacy standards.

Children's Privacy

Vendora does not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 16. If we become aware that a minor has provided personal data, we will take steps to remove that account.

Changes to This Policy

Vendora may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Users are encouraged to review it regularly. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our official communication channels. We strive to respond promptly to all inquiries.
